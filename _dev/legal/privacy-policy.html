Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Gloo ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile puzzle game developed by Gloo Game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Gloo on iOS, Android, or the web.

By using Gloo, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Display Name: An optional username for leaderboards.
  • Game Progress: Scores, levels completed, unlocked items, and settings preferences.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Anonymous Identifier: We use Supabase anonymous authentication to create a unique, non-personally-identifiable user ID for syncing your game data across devices.
  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect aggregated, anonymized usage data such as game mode played, scores achieved, features used, and session duration. This data does not identify you personally.
  • Crash Reports: Firebase Crashlytics collects technical data when the app crashes, including device type, OS version, and stack traces. This data is used solely to fix bugs and improve stability.
  • Advertising Data: If you interact with ads (served by Google AdMob), AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant advertisements. See Google's privacy policy for details.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, or physical address.
  • We do not collect precise location data.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, or microphone.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Game Functionality: Save your progress, sync data across devices, display leaderboards, and enable PvP matchmaking.
  • Analytics: Understand how players interact with the game to improve gameplay, balance difficulty, and prioritize new features.
  • Crash Reporting: Identify and fix technical issues to provide a stable experience.
  • Advertising: Display ads to support the free version of the game. Premium users (Gloo+) may opt out of ads.
  • In-App Purchases: Process purchases through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not handle payment information directly.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Game settings, preferences, and cached data are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.
  • Cloud Storage: Leaderboard scores, PvP statistics, and game progress are stored on Supabase (hosted on AWS). Data is protected by Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring users can only access their own data.
  • Firebase: Analytics and crash data are processed by Google Firebase services. See Firebase Privacy.
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

Gloo integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Supabase: supabase.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: For in-app purchases and app distribution.

5. Children's Privacy

Gloo is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). The app does not require account creation with personal details. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Analytics Opt-Out

You can disable analytics data collection in the app's Settings screen. When disabled, no analytics or crash data will be sent to Firebase.

6.2 Ad Tracking (iOS)

On iOS, the app will request your permission via App Tracking Transparency (ATT) before tracking for personalized ads. You can deny this request or change your preference in iOS Settings.

6.3 Delete Your Data

You can delete all your local data from the Settings screen using the "Clear All Data" option. To request deletion of your cloud data (leaderboard entries, PvP statistics), please contact us at the email below.

6.4 GDPR / KVKK Rights

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or Turkey, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Data Retention

We retain your game data for as long as your account is active. Anonymous analytics data is retained according to Firebase's default retention periods. You may request data deletion at any time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
